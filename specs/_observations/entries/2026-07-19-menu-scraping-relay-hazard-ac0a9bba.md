- 2026-07-19 [planwright] Answering a workers AskUserQuestion by tmux keystroke-counting is a mis-selection hazard. The relay selects a fork option by sending Down-arrows then Enter; the tower must capture-pane and VERIFY the highlighted line before Enter because option ORDER is not stable. On worker-permission-ergonomics Task 1 finding F3 the worker put Skip as option 1 (its recommendation) and Apply as option 2, the reverse of the sibling F1/F2 prompts; a blind Down-Down or default-Enter would have selected the wrong disposition. Verify-before-submit is the working stopgap but is fragile (extra round-trips, partial-frame captures) and does not scale to unattended operation. Deterministic fix: a structured worker-to-tower decision channel (the worker writes the pending decision plus option set to a file/marker the tower reads and answers, not by screen-scraped menu position), the same event-driven direction as hook-based-worker-attention-signal. Ties to the fleet-autonomy attention surface (D-9).
