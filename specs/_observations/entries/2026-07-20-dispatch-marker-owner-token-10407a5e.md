- 2026-07-20 [planwright] fleet-hardening Task 10 (scripts/fleet-dispatch-worktree.sh): the tmux dispatch primitive reconciles same-task collisions via an OWNERLESS timestamp marker (orchestrate-marker) + tmux session. Because the marker carries no owner token, the primitive alone does not serialize two concurrent dispatches of the SAME task: A creates the worktree then (pre-marker) is preempted; B sees no marker/session, classifies A stale, and its reconcile could force-remove A's fresh checkout (data loss). Mitigated in the real flow because /orchestrate records the marker under the per-spec lock BEFORE dispatching, so B sees A's marker and aborts. The durable fix (an owner-token lock discipline across the whole planwright lock family) is already deferred by scripts/fleet-state.sh's stale-break note; this dispatch marker is another consumer that would benefit. Also: a failed attach leaves the marker stamped, so a retry reads already-in-flight until it ages past LIVENESS_TTL (bounded, not permanent).
