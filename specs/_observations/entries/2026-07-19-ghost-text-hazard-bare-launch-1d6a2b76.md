During the worker-permission-ergonomics run, workers launched as bare claude --worktree <suffix> --tmux=classic --model opus --permission-mode auto (bare, to match the claude --worktree allow-rule) DROPPED the CLAUDE_CODE_ENABLE_PROMPT_SUGGESTION=false hardening (D-10/REQ-D1.1), so their input lines repeatedly showed dangerous prompt-suggestions at idle (examples: mark PR ready; clean up worktree and window) which, if submitted by a stray Enter or mis-timed relay, would mark a PR ready or delete a worktree. The tower had to send C-u and replace before every relay. Deterministic fix: (a) fix the tower/adopter scripts allow-rule glob to <dir>/* not <dir>/:* (the word-boundary footgun Task 3 documented for the adopter entry also applies to planwrights own launch path); or (b) a launch shape that both applies the ghost-text pin AND matches an allow-rule.
